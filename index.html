About Me

Hi, I’m Margharyta — a tattoo artist who turns ideas into lasting artwork. My passion lies in capturing emotion, detail, and meaning in every piece I create. Whether it’s a minimalist design or a full-sleeve project, I approach each tattoo with care, creativity, and precision. I see skin as a canvas, and I love helping people express themselves through body art that’s both beautiful and personal.

Art has always been part of my life. Before tattooing, I studied traditional drawing and painting, which shaped the way I work today. I take time to listen, understand your vision, and design something that feels truly yours. For me, every tattoo is more than just ink — it’s a collaboration, a memory, and a small piece of your story.
